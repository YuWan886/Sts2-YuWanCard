## Short Description

YuWan Card is a Slay the Spire 2 mod that adds 23 colorless cards, 5 powers, 12 relics, 1 Ancient One, 1 monster, and an Endless modifier. Features pig-themed cards and unique mechanics like energy doubling and multiplayer support.

## Full Description

[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/YuWan886/Sts2-YuWanCard/main/YuWanCard/mod_image.png[/img]
[b]YuWan Card[/b] - A pig-themed mod for Slay the Spire 2!
[list]
[*][b]23 Colorless Cards[/b] - Including pig-themed cards (Pig Hurt, Pig Think, Pig Angry, Pig Sleep, Pig Sacrifice, Pig Doubt, Pig Slack, Pig Bankruptcy, Pig Brain Overload, Pig Melt, You Are Pig) and unique cards like Rain Dark, Melancholy Is A Feeling, Sad Soldiers Win, and Resurrect![/*]
[*][b]5 Powers[/b] - Pig Doubt (gain random Power each turn), Rain Dark (double energy gained), Pig Bankruptcy, Pig Brain Overload, and You Are Pig[/*]
[*][b]12 Relics[/b] - Seven Curses Ring (Ancient rarity, high risk/high reward), 10 Year Lone Bamboo (Uncommon, stacking Block), Heavenly Hammer, Double Pupils, Supreme Bone, Pride Pig, Envy Pig, Wrath Pig, Sloth Pig, Greed Pig, Gluttony Pig, and Lust Pig[/*]
[*][b]1 Ancient One[/b] - Piggy offers three options: choose a pig-themed card, choose a relic, or upgrade up to 5 cards[/*]
[*][b]1 Monster[/b] - Killer (Elite) with hardened shell and powerful attacks[/*]
[*][b]1 Modifier[/b] - Endless mode: climb the spire endlessly with scaling enemy difficulty[/*]
[/list]

[b]Card Highlights:[/b]
[list]
[*]Pig Hurt - Apply 2 Vulnerable to all enemies[/*]
[*]Pig Think - All players gain 2 energy[/*]
[*]Pig Angry - All players gain 4 Strength[/*]
[*]Rain Dark - Set HP to 6, gain Intangible, double energy, draw full hand[/*]
[*]Sad Soldiers Win - Kill an enemy below 10% HP[/*]
[*]Resurrect! - Revive a dead ally (Multiplayer)[/*]
[*]Pig Brain Overload - Gain 3 energy and draw 2 cards, but receive Stun every 2 turns[/*]
[*]Pig Melt - Gain Buffer and end the turn[/*]
[*]You Are Pig - Turn a teammate into a pig with Buffer and Regen[/*]
[/list]

[b]Relic Highlights:[/b]
[list]
[*][color=red]Seven Curses Ring[/color] - Ancient relic with powerful bonuses and severe penalties (+1 energy, +1 draw, +50% BOSS damage, but -50% gold, -20% Block, -50% heal, and more!)[/*]
[*]10 Year Lone Bamboo - Gain Block each turn, bonus Block from Elites and BOSSes[/*]
[*]Heavenly Hammer - Upgrade King's Sword in hand each turn[/*]
[*]Double Pupils - Copy a card from deck at start of battle[/*]
[*]Supreme Bone - Exhaust 2 cards at start of battle, gain energy and draw when HP drops below 30%[/*]
[*]Pride Pig - Take extra damage but deal more to Vulnerable enemies[/*]
[*]Envy Pig - Gain enemy buffs and draw extra card each turn[/*]
[*]Wrath Pig - Gain 4 Strength at start of battle[/*]
[*]Sloth Pig - Gain 2 Dexterity every 2 turns and 50% less chance to draw Attack cards[/*]
[*]Greed Pig - Add Greed card to hand and gain 50% more gold[/*]
[*]Gluttony Pig - Eat card rewards for Armor and Strength[/*]
[*]Lust Pig - Apply Weak to enemies and make them attack each other[/*]
[/list]
