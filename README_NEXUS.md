## Short Description

YuWan Card is a Slay the Spire 2 mod that adds a Pig character, 60+ colorless cards, 7 powers, 4 enchantments, 14 relics, 1 Ancient One, 1 monster, 1 event, an Endless modifier, and 1 orb type. Features pig-themed cards, unique mechanics like energy doubling, multiplayer support, and shopping cart system.
