## Short Description

YuWan Card is a Slay the Spire 2 mod that adds 33 colorless cards, 5 powers, 12 relics, 1 Ancient One, 1 monster, 1 event, and an Endless modifier. Features pig-themed cards and unique mechanics like energy doubling and multiplayer support.

## Full Description

[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/YuWan886/Sts2-YuWanCard/main/YuWanCard/mod_image.png[/img]
[b]YuWan Card[/b] - A pig-themed mod for Slay the Spire 2!
[list]
[*][b]33 Colorless Cards[/b] - Including pig-themed cards (Pig Hurt, Pig Think, Pig Angry, Pig Sleep, Pig Sacrifice, Pig Doubt, Pig Slacking, Pig Bankruptcy, Pig Brain Overload, Pig Melt, You Are Pig, Pig King, Pig Dragon Ride, Pig Crash, Pig Bite) and unique cards like Rain Dark, Melancholy Is A Feeling, Sad Army Wins, Revive!, All In, Steal Sheep, Cut Your Internet, Really Need To Train You, Spirit Speech · Don't Die, Pressuring You, and Moving Stone King![/*]
[*][b]5 Powers[/b] - Pig Doubt (gain random Power each turn), Rain Dark (double energy gained), Pig Bankruptcy, Pig Brain Overload, and You Are Pig[/*]
[*][b]12 Relics[/b] - Ring of Seven Curses (Ancient rarity, high risk/high reward), 10 Year Bamboo (Uncommon, stacking Block), Haotian Hammer, Reincarnated Eye, Supreme Bone, Arrogant Pig, Jealous Pig, Furious Pig, Lazy Pig, Greedy Pig, Gluttonous Pig, and Lustful Pig[/*]
[*][b]1 Ancient One[/b] - Piggy offers three options: choose a pig-themed card, choose a relic, or upgrade up to 5 cards[/*]
[*][b]1 Monster[/b] - Killer (Elite) with hardened shell and powerful attacks[/*]
[*][b]1 Event[/b] - Blacksmith: upgrade or fuse cards[/*]
[*][b]1 Modifier[/b] - Endless mode: climb the spire endlessly with scaling enemy difficulty[/*]
[/list]

[b]Card Highlights:[/b]
[list]
[*]Pig Hurt - Apply 2 Vulnerable to all enemies[/*]
[*]Pig Think - All players gain 2 energy[/*]
[*]Pig Angry - All players gain 4 Strength[/*]
[*]Pig Sleep - End turn, gain 10 Block and heal 5 HP[/*]
[*]Pig Sacrifice - Give 50% of HP and Block to target ally (Multiplayer)[/*]
[*]Pig Doubt - Gain a random Power each turn[/*]
[*]Pig Slacking - Retain hand, gain 2 Energy next turn[/*]
[*]Pig Bankruptcy - All cards cost 0 but have Exhaust[/*]
[*]Pig King - Transform 3 cards into Pig Charge, Pig Barrage, and Pig Shield Break[/*]
[*]Rain Dark - Set all players' HP to 25%, gain Intangible and Rain Dark, double energy, draw full hand[/*]
[*]Melancholy Is A Feeling - Gain Block equal to current HP[/*]
[*]Sad Army Wins - Kill an enemy when HP is below 10%[/*]
[*]Revive! - Resurrect a dead ally (Multiplayer)[/*]
[*]Pig Brain Overload - Gain 3 energy and draw 2 cards, but receive Dazed every 2 turns[/*]
[*]Pig Melt - Gain Buffer and end the turn[/*]
[*]You Are Pig - Turn a teammate into a pig with Buffer and Regen[/*]
[*]Pig Dragon Ride - Deal 7 damage 3 times[/*]
[*]Pig Crash - Lose 2 HP, deal 14 damage to all enemies[/*]
[*]Pig Bite - Apply 4 Poison, 2 Weak, 2 Vulnerable, and 1 You Are Pig[/*]
[*]All In - Randomly play 4 cards from discard pile[/*]
[*]Steal Sheep - Steal a card from ally's draw pile (Multiplayer)[/*]
[*]Cut Your Internet - Make ally be taken over by Vakoo (Multiplayer)[/*]
[*]Really Need To Train You - Deal 4 damage to ally, give them 3 Strength (Multiplayer)[/*]
[*]Spirit Speech · Don't Die - Heal ally for 10% max HP, give 3 Regen (Multiplayer)[/*]
[*]Pressuring You - Make ally lose 1 Energy, you gain 1 Energy and draw 1 card (Multiplayer)[/*]
[*]Moving Stone King - Transform all cards in ally's hand into Boulders (Multiplayer)[/*]
[/list]

[b]Relic Highlights:[/b]
[list]
